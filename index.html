About Us:

Welcome to Mystique Serene, where elegance meets exclusivity. We are dedicated to curating a world of timeless sophistication and refined luxury, designed to inspire and elevate every facet of your lifestyle. At Mystique Serene, we believe in crafting more than just products; we create experiences that celebrate individuality and evoke a sense of beauty and harmony.

Our thoughtfully curated collections showcase bespoke designs and exquisite treasures, each reflecting a commitment to artistry, craftsmanship, and unparalleled quality. From opulent decor to distinctive lifestyle offerings, every detail is carefully considered to resonate with those who seek the extraordinary.

Mystique Serene is more than a brand—it’s a philosophy that reimagines the art of living. We aim to redefine luxury by blending tradition with modern innovation, offering a seamless fusion of timeless aesthetics and contemporary sensibilities. Step into our world and discover a sanctuary where sophistication is not just a choice but a way of life.

Join us in celebrating the essence of elegance and the allure of exclusivity, where your journey to timeless refinement begins.

Frequently asked questions (FaQs):

1. How long does shipping take?
- Shipping typically takes 3-7 business days.
2. What is your return/exchange policy?
- Returns or exchanges are accepted within 30 days of purchase
3. How can I track my order?
- You’ll receive a tracking link via email after your order is shipped.
4. Do you ship internationally?
- Yes, we offer worldwide shipping.
5. What if I receive a damaged or incorrect product?
- Contact our support team within 48 hours for assistance.
6. Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?
- Yes, we offer COD nationwide for your convenience.
7. How much is the shipping fee?
-We offer free shipping for orders within the Philippines above ₱1,000. For orders below ₱1,000, standard shipping rates apply.
8. How long does delivery take within the Philippines?
- Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days for Metro Manila and 5-10 days for provincial areas.
9. What courier do you use for shipping?
- We partner with trusted couriers like J&T Express, LBC, and Shopee Xpress.
10. Can I pick up my order instead of having it delivered?
- Yes, pick-up options are available at select locations. Contact us for details.
